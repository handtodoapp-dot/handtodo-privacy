# Privacy Policy – HandToDo

HandToDo respects your privacy.

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data for advertising or commercial purposes.

Data created in the app (tasks, handwritten notes, entered content) is stored locally on your device and, if iCloud sync is enabled, synchronized using Apple’s iCloud services.  
This synchronization is fully managed by Apple and subject to Apple’s privacy policy.

HandToDo:
- does not require user accounts
- does not use third-party analytics
- does not display ads
- does not share data with third parties

If you have any questions regarding privacy, you can contact us at:
